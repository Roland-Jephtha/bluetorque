About ND WENE

We Are Leading Our Agency for 15 Years

NDWENE is a Nigerian-based engineering solutions provider specializing in construction, repairs & maintenance, diesel systems, supplies, training, asset management, and oil & gas services. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between technical expertise and real-world project needs, delivering results that combine quality, efficiency, and safety.

We understand that industries face two major challenges: executing complex projects on time and building a skilled workforce capable of sustaining operations. NDWENE addresses both. We provide end-to-end technical solutions that keep systems running and projects on track, while also offering hands-on training programs designed to close manpower skill gaps in Nigeria. When you choose NDWENE, you choose a partner committed to delivering engineering excellence while developing the human capital that drives lasting progress.
